## THE 5-STEP PROCESS

### Step 1: Choose Your Club
Browse the verified club directory on Weed Madrid. Filter by:
- District (Centro, Malasaña, Chamberí, etc.)
- Tourist-friendly status
- Ratings (editorial, safety, ambience, location)
- Languages spoken
- Opening hours

**TIP**: Tourist-friendly clubs are specially marked and have English-speaking staff.

### Step 2: Submit Invitation Request
Complete the online invitation request form with:
- Your full name (as on ID)
- Email address
- Phone number
- Preferred visit date
- Number of visitors (each needs separate membership)

**IMPORTANT**: Information must match your ID exactly.

### Step 3: Receive Your Invitation
Invitation codes are typically sent within minutes via email. The email includes:
- Unique invitation code
- Club address and directions
- Opening hours
- What to bring
- Club rules summary

**TIP**: Check spam folder if not received within 30 minutes.

### Step 4: Visit the Club
Present your invitation code and valid ID at the club:
1. Show invitation code (printed or on phone)
2. Present valid government ID or passport
3. Complete membership registration form
4. Review and accept club rules
5. Pay membership fee (typically €20-50)

**REQUIRED DOCUMENTS**: Passport or government ID proving you are 21+.

### Step 5: Enjoy Your Membership
Once registered, you can:
- Access club facilities during opening hours
- Consume cannabis in designated areas
- Use club amenities (lounge, games, etc.)
- Return for future visits during membership period

## REQUIREMENTS CHECKLIST

### Who Can Join?
✓ Adults aged 21 or older
✓ Valid government-issued ID or passport
✓ Invitation from existing member or facilitation service
✓ Agreement to club rules and Spanish law

### Who Cannot Join?
✗ Anyone under 21 years old
✗ Those without valid identification
✗ Anyone unable to agree to club rules

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### How long does the process take?
From invitation request to club access: typically same day. Invitation codes are sent within minutes; registration at club takes 10-15 minutes.

### Is there a cost?
Weed Madrid invitation facilitation: FREE
Club membership fees: €20-50 annually (varies by club)

### Can tourists join?
Yes! Many clubs welcome international visitors. Tourist-friendly clubs are marked on the directory.

### Do I need to speak Spanish?
No. Tourist-friendly clubs have English-speaking staff. Many also speak German, French, and Italian.

### Can I bring friends?
Each person needs their own membership. Submit separate invitation requests for each visitor.

### What if I lose my invitation code?
Contact Weed Madrid support to resend your invitation code via email.

---

## TIPS FOR FIRST-TIME VISITORS

1. **Arrive during daytime** for easier navigation and fuller experience
2. **Bring cash** for membership fee (some clubs accept cards)
3. **Plan your transport** - no driving after consumption
4. **Eat beforehand** - clubs may not have full food service
5. **Start slow** if unfamiliar with products
6. **Respect club rules** and other members
7. **Keep your ID handy** for random checks
