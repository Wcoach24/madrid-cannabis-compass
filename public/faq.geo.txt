## BASICS

### What is a cannabis social club?
A cannabis social club (CSC) is a private, non-profit association where adult members can legally consume cannabis in Spain. Clubs operate under Spanish constitutional rights to private association and personal consumption.

### How are cannabis clubs legal in Spain?
Cannabis clubs operate in a legal grey area. Personal consumption in private is decriminalized under Spanish law. Clubs function as private member associations, not commercial businesses. The Spanish Supreme Court (STS 484/2015) established criteria for legal operation.

### What do I need to join a cannabis club?
Requirements: Valid government ID or passport, proof of age (21+), invitation from current member or facilitation service, agreement to club rules and Spanish cannabis laws.

### How long does approval take?
Most invitation requests are processed within minutes. Same-day access is available for all clubs listed on Weed Madrid.

## MEMBERSHIP

## LEGAL

### Is public cannabis consumption legal in Spain?
No. Public consumption remains illegal and can result in fines. Cannabis consumption is only legal in private spaces, including licensed cannabis clubs.

### Can I buy cannabis on the street legally?
No. Street purchase and sale of cannabis is illegal in Spain. Only private cannabis clubs offer legal access for members.

### What are the legal limits?
Personal possession limits vary by region. Clubs must operate as non-profit associations and maintain member records. Sharing cannabis outside club premises is illegal.

### What happens if I break the rules?
Violations can result in club membership revocation. Serious violations may involve legal consequences under Spanish law.

## MEDICAL

### Can I access medical cannabis through clubs?
Cannabis clubs provide general access for adult members. For specific medical needs, consult healthcare professionals. Some clubs cater to wellness-focused consumption.

### Is medical cannabis legal in Spain?
Medical cannabis products (Sativex) are available by prescription. Cannabis clubs operate separately from the medical cannabis system.

### What consumption methods are available?
Clubs typically offer various consumption methods: smoking, vaporizing, and sometimes edibles. Available products vary by club.
