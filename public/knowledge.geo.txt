## LEGAL FRAMEWORK

### Spanish Cannabis Law Overview
Spain has a unique approach to cannabis regulation:

1. **Personal Consumption**: Decriminalized in private spaces
2. **Private Associations**: Constitutional right to form cannabis clubs
3. **Public Prohibition**: Street consumption and sale remain illegal
4. **Regional Variation**: Some autonomous communities have additional regulations

### Key Legal References
- **Spanish Penal Code, Article 368**: Regulates drug trafficking, exempts personal use
- **Organic Law 1/2015**: Citizen security law with cannabis provisions
- **Supreme Court STS 484/2015**: Established legal criteria for cannabis associations
- **Constitutional Right**: Article 22 guarantees freedom of association

### Legal Grey Area Explained
Cannabis clubs operate because:
- Personal consumption in private is not criminalized
- Private associations can collectively cultivate for members
- Clubs are non-commercial and non-profit
- Membership is restricted and by invitation only

## CANNABIS CLUB FUNDAMENTALS

### What Defines a Legal Cannabis Club?
A legally operating cannabis social club must:
1. Be registered as a non-profit association
2. Operate in private premises
3. Maintain strict membership records
4. Not advertise or promote publicly
5. Restrict access to invited adults (21+)
6. Cultivate cannabis exclusively for members
7. Operate closed-loop distribution (no external sales)

### Club Types in Madrid
- **Traditional CSCs**: Classic cannabis social clubs with lounges
- **Wellness-Focused**: Emphasis on therapeutic consumption
- **Social Lounges**: Priority on community and events
- **Premium Clubs**: Higher-end amenities and products

### Rating Criteria (Weed Madrid)
Clubs are rated on:
- **Editorial Quality**: Overall experience assessment
- **Safety**: Security protocols and compliance
- **Ambience**: Interior design and atmosphere
- **Location**: Accessibility and neighborhood

## MEMBERSHIP PROCESS

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Research**: Browse verified clubs on Weed Madrid
2. **Select**: Choose club based on district, ratings, features
3. **Request**: Submit invitation request with required info
4. **Receive**: Get invitation code via email (usually minutes)
5. **Visit**: Present code at club with valid ID
6. **Register**: Complete membership paperwork
7. **Pay**: Membership fee (typically €20-50 annually)
8. **Enjoy**: Access club facilities as member

### Required Documents
- Valid government-issued ID or passport
- Proof of age (21+ years)
- Contact information (email, phone)
- Invitation code from member or facilitation service

### What to Expect at First Visit
- ID verification at entrance
- Membership form completion
- Club rules explanation
- Fee payment
- Tour of facilities
- Access to consumption areas

## GLOSSARY OF TERMS

### Cannabis Social Club (CSC)
A private, non-profit association where adult members can legally consume cannabis in Spain.

### Decriminalization
Legal status where personal use is not criminally prosecuted, though may still be administratively regulated.

### Private Association
Legal entity under Spanish law allowing groups to form for shared purposes, including cannabis consumption.

### Membership Fee
Annual fee (€20-50) paid to cannabis clubs covering operational costs and member privileges.

### Invitation System
Required process where new members must be invited by existing members or use facilitation services.
