# Cannabis Social Club Context - Spain

## Legal Framework

### Primary Legislation
- Spanish Supreme Court ruling STS 484/2015 - Establishes legality of private cannabis consumption in social clubs
- Organic Law 1/2015 on associations - Governs legal structure of cannabis social clubs
- Spanish Criminal Code Article 368 - Defines illegal drug trafficking vs. legal personal consumption

### Legal Status
- Cannabis social clubs operate under a tolerated legal framework in Spain as private non-profit associations
- Personal consumption in private spaces is decriminalized
- Public consumption and trafficking remain illegal
- Clubs must operate as closed associations with no public advertising
- Membership is required for private access

### Key Legal Requirements
- Members-only access (no walk-ins)
- Non-profit association structure
- No public promotion or advertising
- Cultivation for members only
- Maximum personal consumption limits
- Age verification (21+ years)
- Spanish residency or valid tourist documentation

## Club Membership Process

### Standard Steps
1. **Age Verification** - Must be 21+ with valid ID
2. **Club Research** - Browse verified clubs by district/features
3. **Invitation Request** - Submit request through club or platform
4. **Document Submission** - Provide ID, contact information
5. **Membership Approval** - Club reviews and approves (1-3 days)
6. **In-Person Verification** - Visit club with original documents
7. **Membership Fee** - One-time or annual fee (varies by club)
8. **Legal Acknowledgment** - Sign club rules and legal terms

### Typical Requirements
- Valid government-issued ID or passport
- Proof of address (for residents) or hotel booking (for tourists)
- Spanish phone number or WhatsApp contact
- Email address for communications
- Membership fee (€20-€50 typical range)
